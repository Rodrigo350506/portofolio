Projects

Portugal StreetAttack

Portugal StreetAttack was a project that I decided to create on my own initiative, basically consisted of a web platform where users could post their photos in the form of photos in the form of posts, had their own personal profiles where they could manage their page and could use a search tool to look for other users, the administrators had a panel to manage everything that appeared on the FrontEnd thus creating some security within the website. I structured the site both FrontEnd and BackEnd, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Django and SQLite.

Bling Home Real State

Bling Home Real State is a real estate company that contacted me to design their website, it was a project where I made the website for the real estate company, on the FrontEnd users can see the team and the houses advertised and can search for new houses, employees of the real estate company have their own credentials to enter the BackEnd of the where they have access to various metrics such as sales or can simply advertise or manage new homes on the website.
